About This Page

This is a simple demo webpage to show how HTML works. HTML gives structure, CSS adds style, and JavaScript (not shown here yet) can make things interactive.

Lesson

Webpages are made of different parts:

  • Header – the top section (like a title bar).
  • Navigation – links to different parts of the site.
  • Main Content – where the text, images, or videos go.
  • Footer – bottom area for extra info.
